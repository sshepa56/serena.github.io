Access & Inclusion Work

Inclusion & Access Consulting

I am a passionate advocate for disability inclusion and accessibility, bringing firsthand experience as a woman with a physical disability to my work. I help individuals and organizations design environments, services, and digital platforms that are welcoming, equitable, and fully accessible. I offer workshop trainings and editing services to support inclusive practices, identify barriers, and implement universal design principles. I am committed to amplifying the voices of people with disabilities and translating lived experience into actionable solutions that create meaningful access and opportunities.
